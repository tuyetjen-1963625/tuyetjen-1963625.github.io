About the Creation of Jenn's Drawings

Jenn is an up and coming artist. Her drawings are definitely special. She started this company to provide people with art that is affordable, customizable, and meaningful. Her drawings have made her friends shed tears before, most likely tears from laughing too much. But tears nonetheless. She began drawing when she was just 5 years old, gifting her drawings to family members and friends. However, after so much praise, she decided to share her talent with other. Thus, Jenn's drawings was created.

My timeline

Jenn's Life from the beginning until 10/30/2020

BIRTH

Jenn's full name is Jennifer Tuyet Ngan Nguyen. She is the the daughter of an immigrant mom and immigrant dad. She calls them "mom" and "dad," respectively

BROTHER

One day, her parents brought home a baby boy. He is great. She calls him many names like "John," "Dat," "Jonathan," and of course his given English name, Johnny

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Jenn entered Elementary School when she was five. She cried almost every time they dropped her off at school.

MIDDLE SCHOOL

In middle school, Jenn got better at going to school without crying. She moved to a new school so she did not know many people at first. But, she made it through middle school!

HIGH SCHOOL

Jenn entered highschool when she was 14. She had friends. It was memorable. She wouldn't go back though.

COLLEGE

Jenn is currently in college, pursuing a chemical engineering degree. She is in her second year of college.
